- **Policy Optimization**: Every 60 seconds
- **Continuous Monitoring**: Every cycle

### Optimization Strategy

The MPC solver uses **adaptive objective weights** based on current progress:

- If unemployment is high â†’ Focus more on reducing unemployment
- If GDP is low â†’ Focus more on economic growth
